Jim Carrey.
James Eugene Carrey'" (amezaliwa tar. 17 Januari 1962) ni mshindi wa Tuzo ya Golden Globe, akiwa kama mwigizaji filamu na mchekeshaji bora wa Kikanada-Kimarekani. Anafahamika sana kwa kucheza filamu zake za kuchekesha kama vile "Ace Ventura: Pet Detective", "Ace Ventura: When Nature Calls", "The Mask", "Dumb and Dumber", "Me, Myself & Irene", "The Cable Guy", "Liar Liar" na "Bruce Almighty". Carrey pia amepata mafanikio katika filamu za maigizo kama vile "The Truman Show", "Man on the Moon", na "Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind". Ameshinda tuzo ya Golden Globe mnamo mwaka wa 1999 na 2000. Mnamo mwaka wa 2008, ameigiza filamu ya "Yes Man".
