Michelle Forbes.
Michelle Renee Forbes Guajardo'" (amezaliwa tar. 8 Januari 1965 mjini Austin, Texas) ni mwigizaji wa filamu na tamthilia kutoka nchini Marekani. Huenda akawa anafahamika zaidi kwa kazi zake za vipindi vya televisheni kama vile "Star Trek: The Next Generation", "Homicide: Life on the Street", "24", "Battlestar Galactica", "In Treatment" na "Prison Break."
