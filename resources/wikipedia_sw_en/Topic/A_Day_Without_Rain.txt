A Day Without Rain.
"A Day Without Rain" ni albamu ya Enya, iliyotolewa mwaka wa 2000. Ilishinda tuzo la "Grammy Award":"Grammy Award for Best New Age Album" mnamo 2002. Inajulikana sana kutokana na wimbo wake "Only Time" ambayo ilifanikiwa zana mnamo 2001 ilipotambulika na bomu la "11 Septemba 2001".
