Judith John Mzurikwao.
Judith Mpoli John Antonio Mzurikwao'" known mononymously as Divanji,(born April 4th 1986) is a Tanzanian radio personality, lawyer and business woman. Born and raised in several Tanzanian regions aspiring to be a lawyer but has stuck to being a radio presenter and local enterprenuer.
Judith Mzurikwao was born in Kiabakari a small district in Mara region, Tanzania. She is the daughter of Venerenda Choka a retired nurse and John Antonio Mzurikwao a retired army officer at the rank of Colonel. She has a brother Godwin Mzurikwao born in 1983 and three young sibblings namely, Paulina, Christina and Emmanuela.
