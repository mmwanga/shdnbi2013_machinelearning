I Just Can't Stop Loving You.
"I Just Can't Stop Loving You'" ni jina la kutaja wimbo wa ballad ulioimbwa na Michael Jackson akimshirikisha Siedah Garrett. Wimbo huu umetungwa na Jackson, kiasili ulinuiwa uimbwe pamoja na wanawake vipenzi wa Jackson: aidha Barbra Streisand au Whitney Houston.
